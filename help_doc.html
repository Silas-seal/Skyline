THE SKYLINE DIGITAL         HELP DOCUMETATION

The Overview:  This document helps you understand the basic ways of using th Skyline Digital Software in an online marketing.

Getting Started

First, you will need to create an account at the Skyline Digital by clicking on the signUp button on the logIn scene. The signUp form will be displayed on the right. Fill in all the fields of the form and click register. The register button will not work unless all the form fields are filled so please make sure you fill all of the them before clicking Register. Here an account created scene will show up and you will return to home to start and log into your account using your suggested Username and Password.

Transacting

After logging In you can visit the marketing center to to view and purchase items of your desire. You can also visit the Skyline Accounts center by clicking on the finances button. Here you will be able to manage your account in terms of deposit,transfer,withdraw,balance inquiry and the Statement. You can also edit your profile by clicking on the Profile button.
